perfumes details:

Eclave

(Inspired by Baccarat Rouge 540)

Eclave is a rich, warm, and addictive fragrance that radiates luxury. With its sweet amber warmth and smooth woody undertones, it creates a glowing aura around the wearer. Mysterious yet elegant, Eclave is perfect for evenings, special occasions, and moments when you want to stand out effortlessly. It’s bold, long-lasting, and unforgettable.

Scarzaar

(Inspired by Sauvage by Dior)

Scarzaar is powerful, fresh, and magnetic. It opens with a burst of crisp freshness followed by spicy and woody notes that create a strong masculine presence. Designed for confident men who lead from the front, Scarzaar is versatile — perfect for daily wear or evening events. It leaves a clean, bold trail that commands attention.

Celeste

(Inspired by Bombshell by Victoria’s Secret)

Celeste is vibrant, fruity, and beautifully feminine. It blends sparkling fruity notes with soft floral tones, creating a scent that feels youthful and glamorous. Bright and uplifting, Celeste is perfect for daytime wear, brunches, and special gatherings. It’s playful, confident, and irresistibly charming.

Fiora

(Inspired by Gucci Flora by Gucci)

Fiora is delicate, romantic, and graceful. With blooming floral accords and a soft, elegant base, it captures the beauty of a fresh garden in full bloom. Feminine and refined, Fiora is ideal for women who appreciate elegance and timeless charm. It’s soft yet memorable — a fragrance that speaks gently but leaves a lasting impression.
